Breach of Protection Obligation by Institution of Engineers, Singapore

21 Apr 2016

A financial penalty of $10,000 was imposed and directions issued to the Institution of Engineers, Singapore for failing to implement proper and adequate protective measures to secure its IT system, resulting in unauthorised disclosure of the personal data of more than 4,000 members.
